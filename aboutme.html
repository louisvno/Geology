When it comes to geology there are a lot of interesting subjects but my main interest and specialization is basin research. This mostly means sedimentological processes and environments, structural analysis and petroleum geology. Most of all I like integrating different types of to come to a coherent picture of what happened durring the formation of this basin.

Some of the methods and tools I have used during my studies are; fieldwork, seismic interpretation, sequence stratigraphy, geochemical (sourcerock) data, structural data modelling, analog modelling, numerical modelling and GIS (geographical information systems).
